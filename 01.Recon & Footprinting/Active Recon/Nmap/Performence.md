
---
`-T <0-5>`: Speed
	- `-T 0` / `-T paranoid`
	- `-T 1` / `-T sneaky`
	- `-T 2` / `-T polite`
	- `-T 3` / `-T normal`
	- `-T 4` / `-T aggressive`
	- `-T 5` / `-T insane`

`--min-parallelism <number>`: Frequency

`--initial-rtt-timeout <time>`: Initial probe timeout

`--max-rtt-timeout <time>`: Timeout

`--min-rate <number>`: How many packets should be sent simultaneously

`--max-retries <number>`: Number of retries
